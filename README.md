 Vendor & Brand Performance Analysis

An end-to-end analytics project built using SQL, Python, and Power BI to evaluate vendor and brand performance, uncover profitability drivers, and support data-driven procurement and revenue decisions.

Project Overview

This project analyzes vendor- and brand-level sales, purchase, margin, and inventory data to answer key business questions around:

* Top-performing vendors and brands
* Revenue and margin contribution
* Underperforming SKUs and profitability leakages
* Purchase efficiency and inventory trends
* Vendor concentration and dependency risks

 Tech Stack

SQL – data extraction, cleaning, joins, KPI calculations
  Python – EDA, hypothesis testing, trend analysis, automation
  Power BI – interactive dashboards and executive insights

 Key Outcomes

* Identified high-margin vendors and revenue-driving brands
* Highlighted low-performing products and margin leakages
* Built decision-ready KPI dashboards for procurement and finance teams
* Improved visibility into vendor concentration and purchasing patterns

 Dashboard Highlights

* Revenue, Gross Margin %, and Purchase Contribution
* Vendor-wise and Brand-wise performance ranking
* Inventory turnover and stock risk analysis
* Trend views for sales, purchases, and profitability

 Business Impact

Enabled faster, insight-led decisions in **vendor management, sourcing strategy, pricing, and portfolio optimization**.
